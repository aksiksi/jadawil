Welcome to UAEUSched!

This is an extremely early version of my upcoming project. I've deployed this because of the upcoming early registration period. Please report any bugs to cyph0nik[at]gmail[fullstop]com. Sorry for the bland design!

Instructions

  1. Enter the course codes of the courses you'd like to take in Spring 2013.
  2. Hit the submit button to get a list of upto 15 possible schedules.

Notes

If you get no results, one of the following has occured:

  1. There is a direct conflict between two courses and no alternatives are available.
  2. The scheduler did not find any valid schedules within the 10 second limit.
